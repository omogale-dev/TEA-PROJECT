Our story

Rooted in hills, crafted by hand.

Anvi’s Tea began as a quiet search for leaves that tasted like early mist over the mountains — pure, unhurried, and alive.

Today, every batch is handpicked at peak freshness, shade‑dried in small quantities, and blended in micro‑lots to preserve the character of each leaf.

We partner with growers who nurture their soil, use traditional harvesting techniques, and are steadily transitioning to fully regenerative farming.

Purity

Pesticide‑free leaves, slow processed to protect natural oils and aroma.

Craft

Small batches, hand‑sorted leaves, and thoughtful, minimal blending.

Sustainability
